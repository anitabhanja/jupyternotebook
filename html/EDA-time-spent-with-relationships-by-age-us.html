Who Americans Spend Their Time With?¶

As humans, we have heavily relied on cooperation and social relationships to thrive. Of course, who we spend time with evolves throughout our lifetime.

Using the American Time Use survey data from "Our World in Data" (https://ourworldindata.org/time-with-others-lifetime), we look at how Americans spend their time with at various ages of their life.
